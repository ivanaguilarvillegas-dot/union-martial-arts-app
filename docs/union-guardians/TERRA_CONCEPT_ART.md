# UNION GUARDIANS - TERRA CONCEPT ART
Version: 1.0
Status: Official AAA concept art specification
Scope: Character visual lock for TERRA only

## Purpose
Lock the official concept art language for TERRA for all future pipelines.

## Character Core Identity
- Name: TERRA
- Element: Earth
- Role fantasy: Defensive anchor and frontline protector
- Emotional core: Resilient stability
- Visual promise: Unshakable strength with disciplined calm

## 1. General Silhouette
- Primary shape: grounded rectangle-triangle hybrid.
- Read anchors: broad chest, layered stone-plates, rooted stance.
- Must read as reliable fortress, not slow brute caricature.

## 2. Body Proportions
- Total height: 4.5 heads.
- Shoulder width: 1.55 head widths.
- Torso dominant, heavy lower center.
- Forearms thick but not oversized to comedy scale.
- In-world height: 1.20m to 1.30m.

## 3. Facial Expression Profile
- Default: focused serenity.
- Combat: determined and firm.
- Victory: protective pride.
- Forbidden: angry-monster face or empty expression.

## 4. Eye Design
- Shape: narrow oval with strong top brow.
- Iris outer: moss green.
- Iris inner: amber-earth.
- Pupil: rounded with slight vertical compression.
- Subtle glow only in core states.

## 5. Head Design
- Rounded stone-helm skull design with root-like crest grooves.
- Jaw compact and protective.

## 6. Horns
- No horns in base form.
- Crest ridges can evolve into crown-like earth fins at late stages.

## 7. Armor System
- Stone-ceremonial segmented armor.
- Shoulder bastions, chest lockplate, knee and shin guards.
- Keep mobility channels visible.

## 8. Cloth Elements
- Minimal earth-cloth waist wrap.
- Texture: woven dojo-fiber with dust-weathered edges.

## 9. Materials
- Skin: mineral-organic matte surface.
- Armor: carved basalt-alloy blend.
- Core: amber crystal geode inset.
- Cloth: rugged natural fiber.

## 10. Gloves and Hand Gear
- Built-in knuckle stone plates with controlled edge bevels.
- Hands should read both defensive and supportive gestures.

## 11. Boots and Foot Design
- Heavy grounded feet with layered sole plating.
- Contact frames must feel weighty and stable.

## 12. Emblem
- Union earth sigil integrated in chest lockplate.

## 13. Official Color Palette (HEX)
- Earth Brown: #6D4C41
- Basalt Dark: #3E2723
- Clay Warm: #8D6E63
- Moss Green: #558B2F
- Root Green: #33691E
- Amber Core: #FFB300
- Dust Light: #BCAAA4
- Stone Gray Accent: #757575

## 14. Lighting Direction
- Key: warm-neutral top 3/4.
- Fill: low-intensity cool bounce.
- Rim: muted amber-green edge.

## 15. Shadows
- Medium-hard contact shadows for grounded read.
- Maintain armor layer separation in low light.

## 16. Earth Effects
- Controlled dust puffs, root-ripple lines, pebble impact bursts.
- No volcanic fire crossover in base form.

## 17. Particle System
- Dust motes, small rock chips, root-light streaks.
- Low-frequency persistent particles.

## 18. Official Posture
- Stable wide stance.
- Slightly lowered center with forward protection angle.

## 19. Front View Specification
- Chest lockplate centered.
- Shoulder mass balanced, no asymmetry overload.

## 20. Side View Specification
- Spine curve minimal; posture should feel rooted.

## 21. Back View Specification
- Back plate segmentation readable.
- Root crest line must be visible.

## 22. Combat Pose Specification
- Forearm guard raised, rear fist loaded.
- Pose reads shield then counter.

## 23. Victory Pose Specification
- Ground tap + chest rise.
- Emotion: dependable protector.

## 24. Idle Pose Specification
- Slow breathing, tiny weight shifts, low dust halo.

## 25. Expected Detail Level
- AAA stylized with strong material readability.

## 26. Artistic Style Lock
- Monumental but warm.
- Avoid monstrous stone golem style.

## 27. What Must Not Be Done
- No giant horned minotaur look.
- No hyper-rock texture noise.
- No static statue stiffness in pose.

## 28. Prompt-Oriented Summary
TERRA is a stylized AAA earth guardian: stable, resilient, and protective. Broad grounded silhouette, layered stone-ceremonial armor, amber chest sigil, moss-clay palette, and controlled dust/root effects.
