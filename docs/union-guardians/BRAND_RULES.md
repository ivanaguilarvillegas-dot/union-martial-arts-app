# UNION GUARDIANS - BRAND RULES
Version: 0.1 (Scaffold)
Status: Active - Structure only
Scope: Franchise brand governance and identity protection

## Purpose
Define brand rules to protect identity consistency across product, publishing, merch, events, and partnerships.

- Brand essence and value proposition.
- Voice and tone standards.
- Naming and terminology governance.
- Visual and narrative non-negotiables.
- Licensing and co-brand boundaries.
- Approval process and governance model.

## What Is Not Included Yet

- Final partner usage kits.
- Final global localization nuance matrix.
- Final campaign playbook.
