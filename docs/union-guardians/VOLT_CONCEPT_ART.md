# UNION GUARDIANS - VOLT CONCEPT ART
Version: 1.0
Status: Official AAA concept art specification
Scope: Character visual lock for VOLT only

## Purpose
Define the official visual and production identity of VOLT for concept art and implementation.

## Character Core Identity
- Name: VOLT
- Element: Electricity
- Role fantasy: Burst initiator and precision aggressor
- Emotional core: Determined intensity
- Visual promise: Controlled lightning speed and decisive impact

## 1. General Silhouette
- Primary shape: sharp zig-zag with compact core mass.
- Read anchors: angular crest lines, split-tail spark geometry, charged forearms.

## 2. Body Proportions
- Total height: 4.2 heads.
- Athletic compact torso.
- Fast-read limbs with slightly elongated lower legs.
- In-world height: 1.16m to 1.24m.

## 3. Facial Expression Profile
- Default: alert intensity.
- Combat: focused aggression with control.
- Victory: sharp confidence, no taunt arrogance.

## 4. Eye Design
- Shape: narrow angular almond.
- Iris outer: electric yellow.
- Iris inner: white-gold spark.
- Pupil: tight vertical line.

## 5. Head Design
- Aerodynamic angular head with lightning crest edges.
- Compact jaw, strong brow read.

## 6. Horns
- No horns.
- Crest angles can imply voltage prongs but remain plate geometry.

## 7. Armor System
- Light-medium conductive ceremonial armor.
- Forearm channel plates, chest capacitor frame, shin stream guards.

## 8. Cloth Elements
- Minimal conductive sash strip.
- Short and tightly controlled motion.

## 9. Materials
- Skin: matte-organic with subtle conductive lines.
- Armor: brushed electro-alloy.
- Core: capacitor crystal node.
- Cloth: tight-woven technical fiber.

## 10. Gloves and Hand Gear
- Strike gauntlets with charge line insets.

## 11. Boots and Foot Design
- Traction-focused feet with charge vents near ankles.

## 12. Emblem
- Union lightning sigil centered in chest capacitor frame.

## 13. Official Color Palette (HEX)
- Electric Yellow: #FDD835
- Volt Gold: #F9A825
- Spark White: #FFF59D
- Deep Navy: #1A237E
- Dark Conductive Blue: #283593
- Steel Gray: #78909C
- Impact Amber: #FFB300
- Charge Highlight: #F5F5F5

## 14. Lighting Direction
- Strong key with bright highlights.
- Cool fill.
- Sharp rim accents to stress speed.

## 15. Shadows
- Medium contrast shadows with clear edge shapes.

## 16. Electricity Effects
- Arc bolts, short chain sparks, charge bursts.
- Keep arcs readable and directional.

## 17. Particle System
- Spark motes, ion trails, charge dust.

## 18. Official Posture
- Slight forward attack lean, loaded rear leg, high readiness.

## 19. Front View Specification
- Angular symmetry and clean charge line mapping.

## 20. Side View Specification
- Clear acceleration line through spine and tail split.

## 21. Back View Specification
- Back charge channels and crest silhouette must read instantly.

## 22. Combat Pose Specification
- One forearm charged, opposite side stabilizing centerline.

## 23. Victory Pose Specification
- Sharp upright pose with controlled spark halo.

## 24. Idle Pose Specification
- Subtle jitter-energy pulse and quick micro-adjustments.

## 25. Expected Detail Level
- AAA stylized, high precision readability.

## 26. Artistic Style Lock
- Aggressive precision, not chaotic electricity monster.

## 27. What Must Not Be Done
- No thunder beast cliches.
- No random bolt clutter.
- No full-neon wash over all materials.

## 28. Prompt-Oriented Summary
VOLT is a stylized AAA electric guardian: compact, sharp, and decisive. Zig-zag silhouette, conductive ceremonial armor, yellow-navy palette, and controlled lightning arcs for burst combat identity.
