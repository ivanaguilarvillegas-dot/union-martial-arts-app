# UNION GUARDIANS - AQUA CONCEPT ART
Version: 1.0
Status: Official AAA concept art specification
Scope: Character visual lock for AQUA only

## Purpose
Define the official visual identity of AQUA so any AI or artist can reproduce the same guardian with high consistency.

## Character Core Identity
- Name: AQUA
- Element: Water
- Role fantasy: Defensive tactician and team stabilizer
- Emotional core: Calm under pressure
- Visual promise: Fluid strength and precise control

## 1. General Silhouette
- Primary shape: flowing teardrop + shielded torso mass.
- Read anchors: curved head crest, shell-like shoulder frame, fluid tail-fins.
- Silhouette must read as protective and fluid, never bulky brute.

## 2. Body Proportions
- Total height: 4.2 heads.
- Shoulder width: 1.3 head widths.
- Torso: rounded, compact, center-mass stable.
- Forearms: medium thickness for guard readability.
- Legs: balanced, slightly shorter lower leg for grounded defense.
- In-world height: 1.15m to 1.23m.

## 3. Facial Expression Profile
- Default: serene focus.
- Combat: concentrated calm, no panic distortion.
- Victory: gentle confidence.
- Forbidden: rage-face, cruel grin, fear-chaos expression.

## 4. Eye Design
- Shape: oval-almond hybrid.
- Iris outer: deep aqua blue.
- Iris inner: cyan glow.
- Core spark: soft pearl white.
- Pupil: rounded vertical-oval.
- Glow behavior: low pulse in idle, brighter during shielding actions.

## 5. Head Design
- Rounded head plate with current-like side fins.
- No sharp predatory spikes.
- Crown line must feel like flowing water direction.

## 6. Horns
- No horns in base form.
- Uses water-fin crest geometry only.

## 7. Armor System
- Ceremonial fluid armor with shell-influenced plates.
- Shoulder shields, forearm wave-guards, chest core vessel.
- Armor must look light-medium, not tank-heavy.

## 8. Cloth Elements
- Minimal cloth: one short wave-ribbon at waist.
- Cloth motion is smooth and delayed, never chaotic.

## 9. Materials
- Skin: semi-matte aquatic scale.
- Armor: pearl-metal blend.
- Core: translucent hydro-crystal.
- Cloth: soft woven marine fiber.

## 10. Gloves and Hand Gear
- Integrated wave-gauntlets.
- Fingers are slender but readable for gesture and casting.

## 11. Boots and Foot Design
- Organic armored feet with fin-like ankle plating.
- Ground contact must feel stable despite fluid theme.

## 12. Emblem
- Union water sigil at chest core.
- Optional shoulder micro-emblem for rank variants.

## 13. Official Color Palette (HEX)
- Deep Water Blue: #1565C0
- Ocean Blue: #1E88E5
- Aqua Cyan: #26C6DA
- Foam Light: #B3E5FC
- Pearl White: #EAF6FF
- Deep Shadow Navy: #0D3C61
- Shell Silver: #90A4AE
- Sea Accent Teal: #0097A7

## 14. Lighting Direction
- Key: cool top-front.
- Fill: soft neutral.
- Rim: cyan highlight.
- Keep water materials luminous without plastic look.

## 15. Shadows
- Clean cool shadows with subtle gradient.
- Preserve eye readability and chest emblem clarity.

## 16. Water Effects
- Controlled currents, splash ribbons, micro-droplet halos.
- No tsunami-chaos in idle.
- Shield actions produce curved water walls with clear geometry.

## 17. Particle System
- Fine droplets, foam sparks, ripple motes.
- Low density in idle, medium burst in impact/defense.

## 18. Official Posture
- Balanced low-mid guard.
- Open chest, one forearm slightly forward for defensive projection.

## 19. Front View Specification
- Symmetry in face and chest core.
- Controlled asymmetry in cloth accent only.

## 20. Side View Specification
- Clear fluid spine arc.
- Visible crest-fin silhouette and forearm shield depth.

## 21. Back View Specification
- Back fins and aura anchor ring must be readable.
- Tail-fin structure cannot look fish-like comedic.

## 22. Combat Pose Specification
- One arm casts water barrier, rear arm supports counter-flow strike.
- Stance must read strategy before aggression.

## 23. Victory Pose Specification
- Upright, hand over core, soft rising water halo.
- Emotion: composed gratitude.

## 24. Idle Pose Specification
- Subtle breathing + floating water orbit near forearms.
- 4.8s to 6.2s loop target.

## 25. Expected Detail Level
- AAA stylized with clear mid-distance readability.
- Layer hierarchy: silhouette, material contrast, emblem, micro details.

## 26. Artistic Style Lock
- Heroic-stylized, fluid, elegant, disciplined.
- Never cartoon-slapstick, never photoreal fish-creature.

## 27. What Must Not Be Done
- No shark monster redesign.
- No oversized mermaid tail shapes.
- No heavy dark saturation that kills calm identity.
- No chaotic water FX obscuring silhouette.

## 28. Prompt-Oriented Summary
AQUA is a stylized AAA water guardian: calm, precise, protective. Rounded-fluid silhouette with defensive forearm wave-guards, hydro-crystal chest emblem, cyan-blue palette, controlled water effects, and disciplined posture.
