# UNION GUARDIANS - UMBRA CONCEPT ART
Version: 1.0
Status: Official AAA concept art specification
Scope: Character visual lock for UMBRA only

## Purpose
Define the official concept art identity for UMBRA as a disciplined shadow guardian.

## Character Core Identity
- Name: UMBRA
- Element: Shadow
- Role fantasy: Tactical disruptor and control specialist
- Emotional core: Silent self-mastery
- Visual promise: Elegant mystery with strategic precision

## 1. General Silhouette
- Primary shape: crescent cloak-line with feline core structure.
- Read anchors: low-profile head crest, cloak-like dorsal silhouette, refined forearms.

## 2. Body Proportions
- Total height: 4.25 heads.
- Lean torso with stable hips.
- Limbs medium-long for stealth and control poses.
- In-world height: 1.15m to 1.24m.

## 3. Facial Expression Profile
- Default: composed and unreadable calm.
- Combat: focused minimal expression.
- Victory: restrained confidence.

## 4. Eye Design
- Shape: narrow almond.
- Iris outer: muted violet.
- Iris inner: pale silver glow.
- Pupil: thin vertical slit.

## 5. Head Design
- Smooth shadow-crest, minimal protrusions.
- High brow shadow line for strategic read.

## 6. Horns
- No horns.
- Any upward forms must remain soft crest geometry.

## 7. Armor System
- Lightweight shadow-ceremonial plates.
- Forearm precision guards and chest sigil frame.
- Armor profile is elegant, not bulky.

## 8. Cloth Elements
- One short veil-ribbon or mantle fragment.
- Motion should be subtle and delayed.

## 9. Materials
- Skin: low-sheen matte surface.
- Armor: dark satin alloy.
- Core: dim silver-violet lens.
- Cloth: soft deep-fiber shadow weave.

## 10. Gloves and Hand Gear
- Tapered control gloves with clean finger articulation.

## 11. Boots and Foot Design
- Silent-step feet with narrow stable base.

## 12. Emblem
- Union shadow sigil placed on chest frame, low emissive.

## 13. Official Color Palette (HEX)
- Void Violet: #5E35B1
- Night Indigo: #283593
- Smoke Gray: #455A64
- Shadow Black: #121730
- Silver Mist: #B0BEC5
- Lunar Accent: #9575CD
- Deep Charcoal: #263238
- Soft Glow Lilac: #D1C4E9

## 14. Lighting Direction
- Low-key controlled lighting.
- Strong silhouette rim for readability.

## 15. Shadows
- Layered shadow gradients that preserve form.
- Avoid flat black crush.

## 16. Shadow Effects
- Penumbra veils, low smoke trails, eclipse rings.
- Effects should conceal intent, not silhouette.

## 17. Particle System
- Fine dark motes, silver flickers, soft shadow wisps.

## 18. Official Posture
- Centered low-ready stance, minimal movement waste.

## 19. Front View Specification
- Face and chest sigil must read despite dark palette.

## 20. Side View Specification
- Cloak-line and tail profile define identity.

## 21. Back View Specification
- Dorsal shadow mantle silhouette is mandatory anchor.

## 22. Combat Pose Specification
- One hand in control gesture, one in strike-ready line.

## 23. Victory Pose Specification
- Subtle composed stance with shadow ring pulse.

## 24. Idle Pose Specification
- Slow breathing and low-amplitude mantle sway.

## 25. Expected Detail Level
- AAA stylized with premium shadow material control.

## 26. Artistic Style Lock
- Strategic elegance over intimidation.

## 27. What Must Not Be Done
- No villain-coded demonic spikes.
- No horror creature face.
- No full-black detail loss.

## 28. Prompt-Oriented Summary
UMBRA is a stylized AAA shadow guardian: elegant, controlled, and strategic. Crescent cloak silhouette, dark satin armor, violet-indigo palette, restrained shadow FX, and disciplined tactical posture.
