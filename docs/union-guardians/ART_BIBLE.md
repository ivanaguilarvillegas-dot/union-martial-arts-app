# UNION GUARDIANS - ART BIBLE
Version: 0.1 (Scaffold)
Status: Active - Structure only
Scope: Visual identity and production standards

## Purpose
Establish the master visual language for characters, creatures, environments, effects, and marketing assets.

## What This Document Will Contain
- Visual philosophy and emotional targets.
- Shape language and silhouette rules.
- Color hierarchy and palette governance.
- Materials, lighting, and rendering direction.
- Environment art standards.
- Asset consistency and review process.

## What Is Not Included Yet
- Final concept sheets.
- Final turnaround packs.
- Final material libraries.
- Final production paintovers.

## Development Stages
1. Stage A: Core visual identity.
2. Stage B: Character and environment standards.
3. Stage C: FX, materials, and lighting rules.
4. Stage D: Production pipeline quality gates.
