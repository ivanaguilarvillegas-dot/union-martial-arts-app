# UNION GUARDIANS - SOLIS CONCEPT ART
Version: 1.0
Status: Official AAA concept art specification
Scope: Character visual lock for SOLIS only

## Purpose
Define the official visual identity of SOLIS for all concept and production use.

## Character Core Identity
- Name: SOLIS
- Element: Light
- Role fantasy: Team inspirer and radiant support leader
- Emotional core: Hope in action
- Visual promise: Warm heroic radiance with noble restraint

## 1. General Silhouette
- Primary shape: radiant crown-circle over stable guardian body.
- Read anchors: antler-like light crown, elegant chest line, luminous tail accents.

## 2. Body Proportions
- Total height: 4.35 heads.
- Balanced torso with graceful shoulder line.
- Legs stable and noble.
- In-world height: 1.18m to 1.28m.

## 3. Facial Expression Profile
- Default: gentle confidence.
- Combat: protective determination.
- Victory: uplifting calm joy.

## 4. Eye Design
- Shape: open almond with warm upper curve.
- Iris outer: golden amber.
- Iris inner: pale sun glow.
- Pupil: rounded vertical oval.

## 5. Head Design
- Smooth noble head frame with radiant crown structures.
- Crown should read symbolic, not literal antlers of a deer clone.

## 6. Horns
- Not true horns.
- Uses radiant crown branches made of light-ceremonial geometry.

## 7. Armor System
- Light ceremonial armor with sunline engravings.
- Chest, shoulders, and forearms hold key radiant accents.

## 8. Cloth Elements
- Short mantle ribbon with sunrise gradient accents.

## 9. Materials
- Skin: semi-matte warm surface.
- Armor: satin gold-alloy with ivory inserts.
- Core: luminous light-crystal.
- Cloth: soft noble weave.

## 10. Gloves and Hand Gear
- Refined support gauntlets with circular motif lines.

## 11. Boots and Foot Design
- Clean stable feet with light-channel trim near ankles.

## 12. Emblem
- Union light sigil centered at chest with controlled glow.

## 13. Official Color Palette (HEX)
- Solar Gold: #FFD54F
- Warm Gold: #FFC107
- Ivory Light: #FFF8E1
- Dawn Orange: #FFB74D
- Noble Cream: #FFE0B2
- Deep Warm Shadow: #6B5B2A
- Soft White Glow: #FFFDF7
- Accent Amber: #FFCA28

## 14. Lighting Direction
- Warm key light with soft bloom control.
- Gentle fill, bright but not washed.

## 15. Shadows
- Warm medium shadows preserving luminous identity.

## 16. Light Effects
- Halo arcs, radiant pulses, guiding beams.
- Avoid blinding flat white overexposure.

## 17. Particle System
- Golden motes, soft rays, tiny prism dust.

## 18. Official Posture
- Upright open-chest posture with welcoming readiness.

## 19. Front View Specification
- Crown symmetry and face warmth must read immediately.

## 20. Side View Specification
- Crown depth and chest emblem projection clearly visible.

## 21. Back View Specification
- Back glow channels and mantle arc maintain identity.

## 22. Combat Pose Specification
- One arm projecting protective light, one arm guiding allies.

## 23. Victory Pose Specification
- Elevated chest and soft upward halo bloom.

## 24. Idle Pose Specification
- Calm breathing and subtle halo pulse loop.

## 25. Expected Detail Level
- AAA stylized with clean premium readability.

## 26. Artistic Style Lock
- Noble, warm, and inspiring.

## 27. What Must Not Be Done
- No angel cliche wings.
- No over-glow that erases form.
- No overly cute mascot face proportions.

## 28. Prompt-Oriented Summary
SOLIS is a stylized AAA light guardian: noble, warm, and inspiring. Radiant crown silhouette, ceremonial gold-ivory armor, controlled halo FX, and support-leader posture.
