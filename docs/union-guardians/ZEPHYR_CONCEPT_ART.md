# UNION GUARDIANS - ZEPHYR CONCEPT ART
Version: 1.0
Status: Official AAA concept art specification
Scope: Character visual lock for ZEPHYR only

## Purpose
Define the official visual identity of ZEPHYR for concept and production consistency.

## Character Core Identity
- Name: ZEPHYR
- Element: Wind
- Role fantasy: Agile striker and mobility tactician
- Emotional core: Free, inventive confidence
- Visual promise: Velocity with graceful precision

## 1. General Silhouette
- Primary shape: arcing crescent with wing-like extensions.
- Read anchors: aerodynamic crest, light torso, dynamic tail streamers.
- Silhouette must read speed and freedom.

## 2. Body Proportions
- Total height: 4.1 heads.
- Lean torso, long limb rhythm.
- Forearms lighter than IGNIS/TERRA.
- Leg chain elongated for movement readability.
- In-world height: 1.14m to 1.22m.

## 3. Facial Expression Profile
- Default: curious confidence.
- Combat: playful precision, not arrogance.
- Victory: uplifting joy.

## 4. Eye Design
- Shape: slim almond with soft curve.
- Iris outer: sky blue.
- Iris inner: wind-white glow.
- Pupil: narrow oval.
- Fast micro-glint in movement states.

## 5. Head Design
- Streamlined head with airflow crest fins.
- Side feather-fins or ribbon-fins for wind identity.

## 6. Horns
- No horns.
- Air crest must remain smooth and lightweight.

## 7. Armor System
- Light ceremonial armor panels on shoulders, forearms, and hips.
- Armor must never feel heavy or rigid.

## 8. Cloth Elements
- Two short wind-ribbons at waist/back.
- Controlled flutter, readable directionality.

## 9. Materials
- Skin: smooth semi-matte with subtle feather-scale transitions.
- Armor: brushed light-alloy.
- Core: pale sky crystal.
- Cloth: thin but structured wind-fabric.

## 10. Gloves and Hand Gear
- Light finger guards with aerodynamic edge lines.

## 11. Boots and Foot Design
- Narrow but stable feet with wind-fin ankle accents.

## 12. Emblem
- Union wind sigil centered on chest or upper torso plate.

## 13. Official Color Palette (HEX)
- Sky Blue: #64B5F6
- Cloud Blue: #90CAF9
- Mist Cyan: #80DEEA
- Air White: #E1F5FE
- Silver Gray: #B0BEC5
- Deep Wind Shadow: #355C7D
- Accent Teal: #4DB6AC
- Motion Highlight: #F5FBFF

## 14. Lighting Direction
- Bright soft key.
- Cool fill.
- Crisp rim on edges to accent speed.

## 15. Shadows
- Soft-medium shadows to preserve lightness.

## 16. Wind Effects
- Stream lines, pressure arcs, light gust spirals.
- Effects must frame motion path, not clutter it.

## 17. Particle System
- Air motes, feather-light sparks, thin vapor streaks.

## 18. Official Posture
- Light forward-angled stance with dynamic balance.

## 19. Front View Specification
- Symmetry in face/core, asymmetry allowed in ribbon motion shapes.

## 20. Side View Specification
- Clear motion intent from head crest to tail stream.

## 21. Back View Specification
- Back silhouette must remain unique with airflow elements.

## 22. Combat Pose Specification
- Mid-step attack-ready stance with high mobility readability.

## 23. Victory Pose Specification
- Uplifted stance, open gesture, upward wind curl.

## 24. Idle Pose Specification
- Soft sway, micro-shifts, low-intensity wind orbit.

## 25. Expected Detail Level
- AAA stylized clarity with high motion readability.

## 26. Artistic Style Lock
- Agile, elegant, confident.

## 27. What Must Not Be Done
- No angel-wing cliche.
- No bird parody proportions.
- No overbusy ribbon clutter.

## 28. Prompt-Oriented Summary
ZEPHYR is a stylized AAA wind guardian: agile, elegant, and inventive. Crescent-speed silhouette, light ceremonial armor, wind-ribbon accents, sky-cyan palette, and directional gust effects.
